The internet. All of it runs on the same technology a solar storm would fry in minutes. A 2013 study estimated the damage at $2.6 trillion in the US alone. The grid could take years to rebuild. The last time a storm this size hit Earth… we had horses.

Full Script

Many eventually can't open their mouths to eat. Here's what makes it worse: surgery doesn't work. Every attempt to remove the extra bone triggers more growth. The body treats the removal as another injury. And responds by building more. There is no cure. There is no way to stop it. Your own body, quietly, permanently, turning you to stone.
